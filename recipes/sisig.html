Sisig

Description

Pork sisig is a popular Filipino dish. It can be considered as a main dish or an appetizer. The composition of this dish is mainly of pigâ€™s parts such as minced pork meat, ears, and face. Chicken liver, onions, and chili peppers are also added.

Ingredients

  • Pig ears
  • Pig snout
  • Pork belly
  • Water
  • Onions
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Bay leaves
  • Lady's Choice Mayonnaise
  • Sukang Iloko
  • Black pepper
  • Liver spread
  • Calamansi

Steps

  1. Boiling the meat
  2. Grilling the pig parts
  3. Preparing the sisig dressing
  4. Tossing the meat and dressing together
